Terraform¶

Terraform is a tool that lets you do “Infrastructure as Code”, meaning that you can declaratively define the state of your infrastructure and terraform will perform the steps to reach that step. Think of it like the kubernetes or NixOS configuration.

The commands for a deployment are usually the following:

terraform init
terraform plan
terraform apply

To undeploy:

terraform destroy
